independent entrepreneurs who invest in the United States deserve a pathway to permanent residence.

our mission

To influence legislation that will make business immigration possible for E2 Treaty Investors and other independent entrepreneurs.

To achieve immigration reform that leads to permanent residence for independent entrepreneurs who contribute to the US economy by starting US businesses.

agenda

  1. raise awareness among law makers, communities and influencers of the important contributions E2 Investor businesses of different shapes and sizes make to the US economy.

  2. lobby for immigration reform bills and amendments that facilitate merit based immigrant visas for independent entrepreneurs who make substantial capital investments and/or hire US workers. View proposed amendments Skills Visa Act

  3. gain support from political leaders to include foreign owned independent US businesses, including E2 Treaty Investors in immigration reform.
